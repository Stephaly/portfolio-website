Stephanie Boyd

I’m a seasoned production designer and researcher, and fledgling UX designer.

I was never particularly artsy, but I’ve always been fascinated by interpersonal communication, so learning the psychology of design and how to communicate with it was a natural fit for me. The research required for UX design is a logical progression of those interests.

Projects

W3

W3 is a weather & water forecasting tool that provides future and current conditions, as well as local info, for both casual and serious water enthusiasts. Design and content decisions were based on conventional UX research methods and tools. Prototypes were constructed in Balsamiq, Sketch, and Adobe XD.

Texas Saltwater Fishing Magazine

TSF Mag is a niche publication featuring tips, stories, and facts about fishing the Texas Gulf Coast. Website and social media offer additional events and opportunities for users to communicate with staff (and vice versa). The magazine is built on a template in Adobe InDesign and uploaded to the printer via Portal. The website is maintained through a custom content management system.
